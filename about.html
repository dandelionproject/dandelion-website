About Us

Our mission, goals, and programs

Our Mission

The Dandelion Project is a student-run organization at Dartmouth College. We focus on bridging the gap in education and social inequality to help students from low-resource and rural schools in China.

Our Organization

The organization was founded by two Dartmouth students in 2012, and has since sent numerous student volunteers to the Dandelion Middle School to teach English classes, organize activities, and provide social and academic support for students. As of 2015, The Dandelion Project is in the process of refocusing to an enhanced model that emphasizes sustainability and experiential learning. More information about our progress can be found here.

Our Vision

We envision that all students, regardless of socioeconomic status or country of origin, should have access high-quality education. More specifically, our organization is focused on bridging this gap by enhancing English learning in Chinese classrooms that request it.
